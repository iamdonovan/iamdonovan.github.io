undergraduate dissertation projects

undergraduate dissertation projects#

Below, you will find some example undergraduate dissertation projects that I have developed/supervised. These projects are largely applying remote sensing to study different topics within geography or environmental sciences.

They are meant to be rather general - these are big topics, with many opportunities to develop additional ideas within the scope of the project. This could include comparing different study areas, investigating different aspects of mapping or detection (i.e., comparing different methods), or looking at changes over time.

  • mapping glacier changes over time
  • detection of supraglacial lakes and timing of drainage
  • mapping harmful algal blooms using satellite remote sensing
  • flood detection and monitoring using remote sensing
  • detection and mapping of wildfires
  • urban heat islands
